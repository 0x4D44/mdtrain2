### Deviations accepted (lead review)
1. **terrain `BALLAST_DROP = ŌłÆ0.4`** (signed). O8 (`terrainHeight==formation+BALLAST_DROP`)
   + O8b (`formation Ōēź terrainHeight`) are jointly satisfiable only if Ōēż0. Ground/ballast
   shoulder sits 0.4 m below rail top (rail proud of ballast). *Instructed at kickoff.*
2. **terrain tunnel BORE-CARVE (significant, agent-originated, ACCEPTED).** The literal D27
   formula `terrainHeight=lerp(normGround,natural,m(s))` is d-independent, so inside a
   tunnel band (natural = hilltop, ABOVE formation) it puts the hilltop at `d=0` near the
   track ŌĆö which (a) violates O8b ("track never below ground"), and (b) would make
   `clampEye` teleport the eye ~60 m up onto the hilltop inside the tunnel. This is a **real
   internal inconsistency in the HLD** between the D27 formula and O8b/clampEye for tunnel
   bands. The implementer resolved it correctly: a smooth, macro-only carve pulls the
   near-track corridor (|d|ŌēżSHOULDER=4, entirely within the render-omitted |d|<BORE_HALF=6
   corridor) back to the bed = the bore floor ŌĆö the physical "the line BORES the hill"
   model. Predicates still read the UN-carved hill (so `tunnelBoreAt` fires); no cliff
   (dense ╬ös=1 O8-edge sweep across both bands passes). **Lead verdict: correct and
   necessary** ŌĆö to be adversarially re-verified in REVIEW (smoothness, predicate-firing,
   clampEye sanity).
3. **terrain O-DOMAIN bound** loosened from `<1` to `<2┬Ęeps+0.1` for `planarPoseAt.x/z` at
   the ends ŌĆö the spine genuinely advances ~1 m per 1 m ╬ös (continuous motion, not a jump).
   The `0.1` constant looks loose; **flagged for REVIEW** to confirm it's not masking a real
   discontinuity (recommend tightening to a jump-style bound).
4. centerline O3 C0 test `eps=1e-8` (slope contribution Ōēł1.2e-10 < 1e-9; measures the
