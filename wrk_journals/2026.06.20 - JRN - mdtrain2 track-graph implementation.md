## DECISION D-IMPL-2 — how to deliver Arthur's "upgrade kingshaven, merge them together"

Area F's reader recommended NO-GO on `sliceRoute` — but its reasoning conflated two things. The
decisive correction: **cross-edge geometry continuity IS solved by `frame = exitFrame(parent)` (the
EdgeFrame keystone, O5b/P1).** So decomposing KINGSGATE into edges threaded by `exitFrame` reproduces
its geometry exactly; the slice only carves the 1-D *data*, and the real hazards are purely 1-D band
straddles (a signal whose `protects` station ends up across the cut; AWS-magnet/OSS underflow near a
cut; a station ±platformHalf or a viaduct/tunnel band truncated by a cut). Those are **avoidable by
construction**: cut only on κ=0 straights in station/signal/band-free windows.

**The call (autonomous, journaled):**
1. **Synthetic testbed + ALL §5 oracles = the signed-off DoD, built verbatim.** Non-negotiable spine.
2. **Live game = "KINGSGATE Junction":** a guarded, oracle-tested `sliceRoute(route, sFrom, sTo)` that
   **asserts** each cut is clean (κ=0; clear of every station±platformHalf, every signal post + its
   derived AWS-magnet(−180)/OSS(−50) reach, every viaduct/tunnel band) and **throws** on a hazardous
   cut — making the decomposition safe by construction. Decompose KINGSGATE at clean cut points in the
   `[5500,9200]` κ=0 straight, thread frames by `exitFrame`, author a **passing loop** + a **branch**
   as fresh Route literals rejoining via P2-enforced merge geometry, run a **looped AI** (player
   overtakes — S1 gameplay) and a **branch AI** (S2). Wire `main.ts` to this graph as the live default;
   `?scenario=testbed` selects the synthetic oracle graph.
3. *Rationale:* honours Arthur's explicit wish (the real 14 km route, upgraded — not discarded, not a
   bare testbed); additive over the signed-off engine (same modules, same oracles); the keystone
   guarantees geometry continuity; the guard makes slicing safe.
4. *Fallback:* if KINGSGATE-junction geometry/integration proves too risky this increment, the

   staged/parked and ship the engine. Aim: complete both.
